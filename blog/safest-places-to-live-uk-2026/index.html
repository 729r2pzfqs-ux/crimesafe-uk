Safest Places to Live in the UK 2026

Looking for a safe place to live? Here are the UK's safest cities and towns based on official police data.

Top 10 Safest Places in the UK

1. Hart, Hampshire

Score: 94/100

Consistently ranked as the UK's safest place to live, Hart offers rural charm with excellent transport links to London.

2. Ribble Valley, Lancashire

Score: 92/100

Beautiful countryside, strong community spirit, and one of the lowest crime rates in England.

3. Rutland

Score: 91/100

England's smallest county offers big benefits: low crime, excellent schools, and stunning natural beauty.

4. South Cambridgeshire

Score: 90/100

Close to Cambridge's opportunities while maintaining village tranquility and safety.

5. Stratford-on-Avon

Score: 89/100

Shakespeare's birthplace is also one of the safest: historic charm meets modern safety.

What Makes a Place Safe?

Our safety scores consider multiple factors:

  • Crime rate per 1,000 residents - Normalised for population size
  • Types of crime - Violent vs property crime
  • Trends - Is crime increasing or decreasing?
  • Detection rates - How effective is local policing?
